CHALLENGE


SourceMedia came to Next Idea Tech with the challenge of customizing their current email management system, SailThru, to perform a specific task. Their goal was to launch an email marketing campaign designed to re-engage audience members by subscribing them to new mailing lists that were relevant to their brand. SailThru itself did not contain the desired feature.

SourceMedia’s required configuration was in abstract stages of development when brought to Next Idea Tech. The client had an idea of what they wanted, and it was up to Next Idea Tech to work closely with SourceMedia to develop the steps to get them from from “raw” idea, to actualized product.


NEXT IDEA TECH SOLUTION


Next Idea Tech collaborated closely with SourceMedia during their “discovery” phase to outline necessary tasks, and to narrow down ideas and project goals. During this phase, it was uncovered that there was a lack of defined target email lists, which would prevent effective distribution of content to subscribers. Next Idea Tech sought to utilize Google spreadsheets to track addresses, and developed an application to review the sheets and send emails accordingly. By defining a thorough project scope for the client, Next Idea Tech was able to jumpstart initiatives and offer streamlined alternatives to inefficient functions.

After close examination of SailThru’s API, Next Idea Tech saw a clear path to reconfigure the software to perform the client’s desired function. Once the Next Idea Tech team had a full understanding of the software, the implementation process was very straightforward given the team’s industry expertise and competitive experience.


RESULTS


Next Idea Tech offered a client-centered approach to problem solving, resulting in a successful project user satisfaction.

The client was able to capture the addresses desired from subscribers through their email marketing campaign. With SailThru’s new configuration, SourceMedia saved significant amounts of manpower that would have been devoted to cross-referencing spreadsheets, managing consumer data, and preventing duplicate emails.

Next Idea Tech’s solution also positively impacted SourceMedia’s email campaign subscribers, resulting in increased engagement, offers, and conversions.
