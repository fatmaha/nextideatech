ABOUT NEXT IDEA TECH

Next Idea Tech is a rapidly growing IT & ITES company, with several accomplishments in a short span. Since 2010, Next Idea Tech has steadily expanded its service offerings across industry sectors, customers, and operating locations, as well as its capability for value creation. Strong leadership, investment in infrastructure and people, customer confidence in our services and our unwavering passion for excellence have significantly contributed to this momentum. Next Idea Tech is constantly investing in state-of-the-art infrastructure, building people competencies, adopting leading-edge technologies, building value for its customers and in everything else essential to achieve customer delight and employee jubilation.

Next Idea Tech provides software services and solutions to mid-sized enterprises globally through our offices in the US, UK and India. We leverage the latest technology stacks in Cloud, Mobile, Business Intelligence and other Enterprise technologies helping clients to get the best value of their IT budget. With our in-house programming studio and a strong team of seasoned professionals, we have implemented 220+ successful projects across 12 countries. Our process-driven approach has helped to achieve ISO 27001. Our team works on .NET, Java, PHP, iOS, Android and other technologies.
