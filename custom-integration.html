PROFESSIONAL SOFTWARE CONSULTING

At Surge, we take software consulting projects very seriously. We want to help you realize your idea, in the most efficient, and cost-effective way possible. Our staff is trained to help you get the most out of your software development budget while also making sure the software project you get delivered to you is the same as what it was intended to be.

CONSUTIN TAILORED TO YOU

Software consulting can come in many forms, and there is no one “right” way to apply it to any project. This is why, at the outset of any project, Surge takes the time to not only get to know your idea, but also your company and your industry.

It is not good enough to just be able to write elegant code, we want to be able to understand what we are building and why. This allows us to offer advice along the way to ensure that you get the maximum benefit from your software project.

TECHNOLOGY CHOICES MATTER

Often, when engaging a software consulting company, clients (whether they know it or not) will be sold whatever solution the company builds the most. Sometimes this is a good fit for the project, other times, though, a different platform would have been a better choice.

Just like choosing any tool, the key for us at Surge is to understand what we are trying to build before we make technology recommendations. Plus, due to the diversity of expertise at Surge, we have experts on almost every platform out there. No matter whether your project calls for a Windows/.Net solution, or would work better on Native Mobile (e.g. iOS or Android), we have all of the technology cases covered.
